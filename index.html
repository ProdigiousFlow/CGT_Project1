About Us

The Guilty Gear: Strive Resource Hub is a place designed to help beginners to Guilty Gear and the fighting genre by giving them ample resources to get into the game and more deeply understand the intricate mechanics the game has to offer for a deeper and more enjoyable experience. Here you will find guides with combos, gameplay advice, and important information regarding characters in the roster that are good for beginners. You can also find guides for fighting games as a whole for people who are entirely new to fighting games and need that extra help. The discord is also available for more personal advise.
