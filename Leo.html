Leo Whitefang

An all-rounder who wields twin swords, turning his back to his opponent as he fights. The Second King of the United Kingdoms of Illyria. He may come off as wild and arrogant, but he's a surprisingly cautious person. He hates losing and holds himself in high esteem. He spares no effort when solving problems. He's made his own personal dictionary, and makes a hobby of defining existing words with actual people and events. Leo's backturn stance can be used to mix up your opponent and catch them out with massive damage.
